How it Works

Follow three simple steps to get your meals without standing in long queues.

1. Place Order Online

Browse the daily catalog, search for foods by category, customize items, and place your order using Cash or Online Payment.

2. Get Token Number

Once paid, a unique order token (e.g. T001) is automatically generated. Keep your ticket secure on your screen or dashboard.

3. Track & Pick Up

Monitor your token status on the tracking page or cafeteria display board. Pick up your meal when the status changes to Ready.
