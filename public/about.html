About Our System

Elevating the university dining experience through technology.

Solving Real Campus Problems

Cafeterias are the heart of university life. However, long queues during lunch hours lead to frustration, missed lectures, and inefficient management.

The **Smart Food Ordering and Token Generation System** was built as a solution. By allowing students and faculty to order online, secure a slot via a real-time token mechanism, and pick up food when it's ready, we completely eliminate waiting times.

Key Project Goals

  • Zero Waiting: Reduce physical queue congestion in food courts.
  • Transparent Tracking: Real-time token updates on prepare/ready phases.
  • Admin Control: Streamlined order updates and sales analytics for canteen staff.
